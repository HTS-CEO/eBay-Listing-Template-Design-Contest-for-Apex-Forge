TECHNICAL DETAILS

Our 3D printed intake system utilizes advanced additive manufacturing techniques to create complex internal geometries that would be impossible with traditional manufacturing methods. The main intake body is printed from carbon fiber reinforced nylon (CFRN) for exceptional strength-to-weight ratio.

The system features:

  • Optimized airflow path with computational fluid dynamics (CFD) analysis
  • Integrated velocity stack for smooth airflow transition
  • Heat shield to isolate from engine bay temperatures
  • High-flow oiled cotton gauze filter (washable/reusable)
  • All stainless steel mounting hardware included
  • Pre-installed mounting grommets for vibration isolation

Each intake is printed with a 0.1mm layer resolution for smooth internal surfaces that minimize turbulence and maximize airflow efficiency. The carbon fiber reinforcement provides dimensional stability under extreme temperatures while remaining 45% lighter than comparable aluminum systems.
